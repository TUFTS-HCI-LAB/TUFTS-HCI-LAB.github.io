      Admissions

      # Undergraduate Admissions

      At Tufts, the admission to undergraduate programs is handled by the Office of Undergraduate Admissions (opens new window). Please visit their website for information about applying to the undergraduate colleges at Tufts. For information about the majors and minors at Tufts, and for general information about Tufts policies and academic requirements, see the Tufts Bulletin (opens new window). Course listings, by department, are available from the Tufts Course Listings (opens new window) website.

      # Graduate Admissions

      Graduate admissions is handled by the Office of Graduate Admissions (opens new window). Information about the graduate programs offered by the Tufts Computer Science department is available from the Computer Science Department website (opens new window). Additionally, some admissions information is also available from the Computer Science Department (opens new window).
